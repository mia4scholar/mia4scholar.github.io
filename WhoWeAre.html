Who We Are

Maracas Capital is named after the beach where the founder, Chris, got engaged. Maracas Capital strives to embody the commitment and faithfulness Chris feels toward his family and values. Just as the beach symbolizes a place of stability and enduring beauty, Maracas Capital is dedicated to offering companies a strong foundation on which they can thrive and grow, with a commitment to long-term partnerships and mutual success.

Maracas Capital is lead by Chris, a US based searcher with an experienced team of entrepreneurs, investors, and current / former CEOs of small and medium-sized businesses with a diverse set of experiences and skills. We know what it takes to foster teams, maintain and enhance culture, and grow businesses for the long-term.
