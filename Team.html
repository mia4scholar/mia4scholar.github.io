Meet The Team
Chris Pitts
Founder & Managing Partner
Chris Pitts is the founder of Maracas Capital and an experienced operator, engineer, and investor with over a decade of leadership across North American manufacturing and project execution. He has led large-scale capital initiatives for Mars, Keurig Dr Pepper, and Dessert Holdings—a Bain Capital portfolio company—delivering multimillion-dollar capacity expansions, packaging transformations, and technical project execution across food and beverage manufacturing.
Chris is the Founding President of the Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition (ETA) Club at Washington University in St. Louis, where he earned his MBA with honors, concentrating in Entrepreneurship and Private Capital. He also holds a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from the University of Alabama at Birmingham. Chris is passionate about building high-performing cultures and creating long-term value through small business ownership.
Advisors
Walker Deibel
Advisor - Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition
Walker Deibel is a bestselling author, investor, and educator best known for his book Buy Then Build, which has become essential reading in the ETA space. As an experienced acquisition entrepreneur and founder of the Acquisition Lab, he has helped thousands of operators successfully acquire businesses. Walker brings a deep understanding of search fund strategy, small business operations, and deal execution to the Maracas Capital team. Reggie Binford Jr.
